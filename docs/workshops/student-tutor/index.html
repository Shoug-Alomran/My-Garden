// IMPACT_LOG

Stepping into a teaching role while managing my own studies pushed me to grow in ways I didn't anticipate. I became sharper at explaining complex ideas, more patient with different learning styles, and more deliberate about how I prepared and communicated. Tutoring didn't just help others — it made me better at everything else I was doing at the same time.
