Introduction to Physical Science

SCI101 introduces fundamental concepts in physical science, covering motion, forces, momentum, energy, gravity, fluid mechanics, and thermodynamics through lecture slides and chapter-based study material.

01_DESCRIPTION

SCI101 is an introductory science course focused on fundamental concepts in physics and chemistry and their everyday applications. It covers motion, energy, momentum, fluids, heat, waves, and the basic properties and interactions of atoms while developing scientific-method awareness and introductory problem-solving skills.
