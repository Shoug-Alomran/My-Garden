## Syllabus-Based Study Path

<div class="overview-steps">
  <div class="overview-step">Start with research terminology, paraphrasing, source synthesis, and APA referencing.</div>
  <div class="overview-step">Build Assignment 1 around topic selection, introduction writing, annotation, and conclusion writing.</div>
  <div class="overview-step">Use the midterm guide after reviewing research definitions, referencing, and annotated bibliography work.</div>
  <div class="overview-step">Build Assignment 2 around paragraph research writing, ethics, participant-oriented research, surveys, methodology, results, and discussion.</div>
  <div class="overview-step">Use the final guide for comprehensive research writing review.</div>
</div>
