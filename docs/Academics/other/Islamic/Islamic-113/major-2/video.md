This video explores Islamic economics as a value-driven system where work, wealth, and exchange are tied to conscience, responsibility, and justice. Drawing on the themes from Lectures 6 to 9, it highlights honest earning, stewardship of wealth, sincerity of intention, moderation in consumption, and fairness in transactions, showing that a healthy economy is not built on profit alone, but on ethics, trust, and accountability.
