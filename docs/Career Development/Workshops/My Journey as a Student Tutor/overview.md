# My Journey as a Student Tutor

![Workshop Poster](pics/poster.png)

**Presenter:** Shoug Alomran  
**Workshop:** رحلتي مع تقديم دروس التقوية / My Journey as a Student Tutor  
**Institution:** Prince Sultan University – Writing & Tutoring Center (WTC)

## Introduction

This workshop shares my personal journey as a student tutor at Prince Sultan University.  
I will talk about how I started, how tutoring shaped my academic and personal growth, and what I learned from mentoring other students.

## Who I Am

I am a Software Engineering and Cybersecurity student at Prince Sultan University.  
Alongside my studies, I work as a peer tutor at the Writing & Tutoring Center (WTC), a cybersecurity trainee, and a student leader in different clubs and initiatives.

## My Roles Outside WTC

- Cybersecurity trainee at MDD  
- Course instructor at Qimah  
- Liaison Officer at ACM Student Chapter  
- Member of the Mental Health & Well-Being Club  

## How Tutoring Shaped My Journey

Tutoring helped me:
- Improve my communication and explanation skills  
- Become more confident and organized  
- Learn how to adapt to different students’ needs  
- Grow both academically and personally
