## High-Level Flow

1. **Setting goals** for the workshop and defining what “offensive security” means.
2. **Exploring attack methodologies** and how real attackers approach targets.
3. **Hands-on demonstrations and labs** on finding and exploiting weaknesses.
4. **Detection and assessment** – linking offensive work to practical defense.
5. **Ethical considerations** and responsible use of offensive techniques.
