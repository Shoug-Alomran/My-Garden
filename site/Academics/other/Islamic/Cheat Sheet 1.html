‚ùå 4 Disadvantages of Capitalism - "ICMU"

Remember: "I Can't Make Unity"

  • Imbalance in wealth distribution (rich get richer)
  • Crises and unemployment (economic instability)
  • Merchandise monopoly (big corporations control markets)
  • Unrestricted freedom in earning & spending (no moral limits)

‚ùå Disadvantages of Socialism - "FTP"

Remember: "Fails To Prosper"

  • Fights natural instinct of ownership
  • Treats humans like machines (kills motivation)
  • Poverty results (lack of incentive)
