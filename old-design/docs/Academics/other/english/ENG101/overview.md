## Course Study Path

<div class="overview-steps">
  <div class="overview-step">Start with sentence patterns, parts of an essay, thesis statements, critical reading, and paragraph unity.</div>
  <div class="overview-step">Prepare Essay 1 through classification and categorization, source reading, synthesis, APA use, drafting, revision, and peer review.</div>
  <div class="overview-step">Prepare Essay 2 through evaluating film and art, argument writing, quotation and paraphrase integration, and midterm essay practice.</div>
  <div class="overview-step">Prepare Essay 3 through multimodal composition and process analysis using text, visual, audio, and spatial modes.</div>
  <div class="overview-step">Use the final review to practice writing one complete academic essay.</div>
</div>
